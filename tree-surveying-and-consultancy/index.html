SURVEYING &
CONSULTANCY

We understand that having trees within your property boundary can be both delightful and concerning. We are here to help you manage trees on your property to enhance it and also keep them safe.

WE OFFER THE FOLLOWING SERVICES TO HOMEOWNER AND COMMERCIAL CUSTOMERS

PLANNING PERMISSION

We are fully able take care of any legalities before undertaking work on your protected trees.

TREE SURVEYING

Our fully trained staff will survey your tree and provide you with a full written report of its details.

If you need advice before work is carried out we can assess your tree on-site and make quality informed decisions on site that day.

TREE PROTECTION ORDER

If your tree has a protection order on it, we can help and advise and try to secure planning to undertake any work.
