OUR STORY

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION
AND ECOLOGICAL
AWARENESS ARE AT THE
HEART OF OUR BUSINESS

G.Faire & Co Tree Services is a second generation family business led by George Faire, who has over 15 years experience in tree felling, tree pruning and the management of large and dangerous trees. We are friendly, professional, tidy and reliable and offer a comprehensive range of services that are ecologically focussed, fully insured and carried out to BS 3998:2010.

We have worked with a wide range of clients, including Henry Williams Roads, Reed Construction, Williams Homes, Denbighshire County Council, Plas Heaton Estate, Bodrhyddan Hall and Gwp Coleg Llandrillo. Our customers recommend us to others, demonstrating the high level of satisfaction we deliver. We are passionate about serving our local community of North East Wales and beyond.
